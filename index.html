✦ THE SPIRIT OF EID ✦

About Eid Al-Adha

Eid Al-Adha, the "Festival of Sacrifice," is one of the holiest celebrations in Islam. It honors the willingness of Prophet Ibrahim (AS) to sacrifice his son as an act of obedience to Allah, commemorating devotion, gratitude, and the spirit of giving.

Prayer & Devotion

Muslims gather for Eid prayer, thanking Allah for his blessings and seeking his mercy and forgiveness.

Sacred Sacrifice

The act of Qurbani honors Prophet Ibrahim's sacrifice, with meat distributed among family, neighbors, and the poor.

Love & Giving

Eid is a time of generosity, sharing gifts, spreading joy, and caring for those around us.

Family & Unity

Families unite, bonds strengthen, and communities come together in celebration, gratitude, and shared joy.
